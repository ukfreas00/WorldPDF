WoldPDF Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

WoldPDF ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Android application WoldPDF collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our application.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

WoldPDF is a fully offline application. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Access your contacts
  • Track your location
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Send data to external servers
  • Require internet connection
100% Offline: All operations are performed locally on your device. Your files never leave your device.

3. Information We Access Locally

The app may access the following on your device only to provide functionality:

  • Storage Access: To read and save PDF files and images you choose to process
  • Camera Access: (Optional) To capture signatures or images for PDF creation

All processing happens locally on your device. Files are never uploaded to any server.

4. How We Use Information

The information accessed by the app is used solely to:

  • Convert images to PDF
  • Merge, split, or compress PDF files
  • Add signatures to documents
  • Extract text from PDFs
  • Apply password protection

5. Data Storage

All files processed by WoldPDF remain on your device. We do not maintain any external database or storage of your documents.

6. Third-Party Services

WoldPDF may use the following local libraries:

  • Google ML Kit: For offline text recognition (OCR). Processing is done on-device only.
  • Android System PDF APIs: For PDF rendering and creation

These libraries operate entirely on your device and do not transmit data externally.

7. Children's Privacy

WoldPDF does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please note:

  • We do not provide direct email support
  • For technical issues, please use the app's built-in feedback mechanism
  • This policy is automatically updated with each app release

10. Google Play Store Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Google Play Store Developer Privacy Policy requirements
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
